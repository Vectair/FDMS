Pre-V1 Master Todo
Purpose

This list consolidates the remaining work required before Vectair Flite V1 operational deployment at EGOW.

The target is not public certification at V1. The target is a technically reliable, auditable, professional, usable VCR operations tool that is built in a way that does not obstruct future certification, assurance review, commercial sale, or formal safety-case work.

Flite’s intended position:

A lightweight airfield operations and flight-progress recording system for small aerodromes and Visual Control Room environments that need more structure, traceability and reporting than spreadsheets or ad-hoc Microsoft Office workflows, but do not require a full Copperchase/Redstone-style airport systems suite.
